Here's our story

We started out at our home kitchen, whipping up finger-foods for our
friends' weddings and nieces' birthdays. Everyone loved our food and our
innovative combinations of flavors.

So, what started out as a family operated business quickly grew into a fully-
fledged small business that we operate out of our professional catering kitchen
in Nairobi. The team has also grown to 6 people; we can cater
at events as large as 1,000 people.
